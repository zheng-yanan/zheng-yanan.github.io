I'm currently a postdoc at the department of computer science of Tsinghua University.

In 2020, I obtained my PhD degree from the School of Software, Tsinghua University. Prior to that, in 2015, I received my batchelor's degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University.

I am a former research intern at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). I received Google China Anita Borg Scholarship.

My research interests mainly focus on natural language-based intelligence, which includes deep learning, natural language understanding and generation.

My dissertation research is Theories and Applications of Natural Language-based Dialogue Interaction. I have been studying algorithms, models and technologies using deep learning methods, especially deep generative models, to make dialogue systems behave more intelligently. It mainly includes three aspects: (1) developing algorithms and models to meet the basic requirements of dialogues, such as informative-ness and consistency; (2) exploring high-level cognitive patterns of dialogues, e.g., emotional/sentimental interaction and topic continuity; (3) enhancing interactivity and manipulation of dialogue systems, e.g., controllable dialogue generation and multi-turn dialogue management.
